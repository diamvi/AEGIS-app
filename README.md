### ae·gis <sub>/ˈējəs/</sub>

> *(in classical art and mythology) an attribute of Zeus and Athene (or their Roman counterparts Jupiter and Minerva) usually represented as a goatskin shield.*

### AEGIS is a health and safety intelligence platform that uses past reports, weather, and satelite data around vector prone areas to protect communities, forecast and preempt future outbreaks of organisms that carry pathogens.
***
## Here are some of the features that help local government officials and eagle-eyed residents stay one step ahead of outbreaks:

### Risk Assessment

Dynamically evaluates the risk of outbreaks in your immediate area by analyzing environmental factors like stagnant water, temperature, humidity, and historical health data, all with the help of AI.
***
<img src="/screenshots/recommendations-unified.png" alt="Three screenshots, the first one showcasing recommendations, second one showing the contributing factors as percentages, and the third one showing the explanation for the stagnant water presence contributing factor" height=400 width=auto>

### Actionable Recommendations

Receive personalized, AI-generated guidance; ranging from immediate emergency actions to community prevention strategies—designed to keep you and your community safe.
***
<img src="/screenshots/map.png" alt="Two screenshots, the first one showing a map with multiple location pins. The second one is a risk assessment for a selected location." height=400 width=auto>

### Risk Mapping

Visualizes high-risk zones across the country with a live map that identifies potential hotspots and tracks emerging health threats in real-time.
### National Analysis

Tap any point on the map to trigger an instant AI analysis, providing a comprehensive risk score and localized safety insights for that specific location.
